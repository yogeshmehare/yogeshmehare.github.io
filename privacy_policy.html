Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 22, 2026  |  Last Updated: February 22, 2026

SweetSwan ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SweetSwan mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our App. By using SweetSwan you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

DataWhen / Why
Account credentials – email address, password (hashed on server)Registration & login
Profile information – name, age, gender, bio, interests, education, occupation, languages, lifestyle preferencesOnboarding & profile editing
Photographs – profile photos uploaded from your camera or photo libraryProfile creation & editing
Face verification selfie – a live selfie compared against your uploaded photos using on-device ML Kit face detectionOptional identity verification
Chat messages – text messages exchanged with matched usersIn-app messaging
Payment information – processed exclusively by Razorpay; we receive only transaction ID, order ID, plan selected, and amount paidPremium subscription purchases

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

DataWhen / Why
Device location – GPS coordinates (latitude & longitude) via ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATIONFinding nearby matches; only approximate distance is shown to other users
Push notification token – Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device tokenDelivering real-time notifications for matches, messages, likes
Device & usage data – device type, OS version, app version, crash logsDiagnostics & improving app stability

1.3 Permissions We Request

  • Camera – To take profile photos and face-verification selfies.
  • Photo Library / Storage – To select existing photos for your profile.
  • Location (Fine & Coarse) – To show you nearby users and calculate approximate distance.
  • Push Notifications – To alert you about new matches, messages, likes, and super likes.
  • Internet & Network State – Required for all server communication.

Every permission is requested at runtime. You can deny or revoke any permission in your device settings at any time; however, some features may not work without them.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service – Display your profile to potential matches, enable swiping, liking, super-liking, matching, and chatting.
  • Location-based matching – Show you users within your preferred distance and show your approximate location to others.
  • Face verification – Compare your selfie against your uploaded photos to award a "Verified" badge. Face comparison is performed on our secure server; we do not store the raw selfie after comparison.
  • Payments – Process premium subscription purchases via Razorpay. We store your plan ID, subscription tier, expiry date, and amount paid — never your card or bank details.
  • Notifications – Send you push notifications about new matches, messages, likes, profile views, and super likes.
  • Safety & moderation – Detect abuse, enforce blocks, and maintain community standards.
  • Improve the App – Analyse aggregated, non-identifying usage patterns to fix bugs and develop features.

3. How We Share Your Information

  • Other Users – Your profile information (name, age, gender, photos, bio, interests, approximate location, verification status) is visible to other SweetSwan users. Your exact GPS coordinates are never shared; only approximate distance is displayed.
  • Razorpay – Payment data is transmitted directly to Razorpay for processing. Razorpay's privacy policy governs the handling of your payment information: https://razorpay.com/privacy/
  • Firebase / Google – FCM tokens for push notifications and crash analytics. Governed by Google's privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Law enforcement – We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Account data is stored on our secure servers (AWS DynamoDB) with encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Passwords are hashed on the server using industry-standard algorithms and are never stored in plain text.
  • Local data (profile cache, preferences) is stored in Android SharedPreferences on your device.
  • Chat messages are transmitted over WebSocket (WSS) and stored server-side.
  • Photos are stored on our cloud storage with access-controlled URLs.

5. Data Retention

  • Active accounts – Data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • Deleted accounts – When you delete your account (Settings → Delete Account), all personal data — profile, photos, matches, conversations, likes — is permanently removed from our servers within 30 days.
  • Face verification selfies – Selfies are used only for the one-time comparison and are not permanently stored.
  • Chat messages – Retained while both participants have active accounts. Messages are deleted when either party deletes their account.

6. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access & edit – View and update your profile data at any time via Settings → Edit Profile.
  • Delete account – Permanently delete all your data via Settings → Delete Account.
  • Revoke permissions – Manage camera, location, notification, and storage permissions in your device settings.
  • Block users – Block any user to prevent them from seeing your profile or contacting you.
  • Incognito mode – Platinum subscribers can browse in incognito mode, hiding their profile from non-matched users.
  • Opt out of notifications – Disable push notifications via Settings → Notifications or your device settings.

7. Children's Privacy

SweetSwan is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If we learn that we have collected data from a minor, we will promptly delete it.
