Privacy Policy — SweetSwan

Last updated: 2026-01-17

Welcome to SweetSwan ("we", "our", "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose and keep your information when you use the SweetSwan mobile application (the "App") and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number (if provided), profile photos, age, gender and bio.
  • Profile content: photos, interests, location preferences and other profile fields you add.
  • Messages: text and media you send to other users in-app.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage information: pages/screens visited, features used and interaction patterns to help improve the App.
  • Device & diagnostic information: device model, OS version, crash logs and performance metrics.
  • Location data: if you grant location access we may collect approximate location to show nearby profiles and calculate distances.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the App, including profile discovery, messaging, matching and notifications.
  • To personalize your experience and show more relevant profiles.
  • For analytics, debugging and improving our service.
  • To send transactional communications related to your account (e.g., password resets, critical notices).

3. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Other users: limited profile information (name, age, photos, gender, location proximity) is visible to other users according to app features and your settings.
  • Service providers: hosting, analytics, push notifications and other third-party services that help operate the App.
  • Legal requests: when required by law or to protect rights, safety or property.

4. Push Notifications

We may use push notifications (FCM or platform push) to notify you about likes, matches and messages. You can manage notification preferences in the app settings or via your device settings.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as needed to provide the service, fulfill legal obligations, resolve disputes or enforce our agreements.

6. Third‑Party Links

The App may include links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information but cannot guarantee absolute security. Please secure your device and account credentials.

8. Children

The App is not intended for users under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

9. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your account information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@sweetswan.example (replace with your real support email).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will post the updated policy here with a revised "Last updated" date.
